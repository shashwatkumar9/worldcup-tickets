## Article Requirements (All Articles)

### SEO Structure
- **Word Count:** Minimum 1500 words, optimal 1800-2000 words
- **H1:** Must include target keyword (e.g., "[Team/Fixture/Venue] World Cup 2026 Tickets")
- **H2-H4:** Logical hierarchy with keyword variations
- **Meta Title:** 55-60 characters, include primary keyword
- **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters, compelling CTA, include keyword
- **URL Slug:** Clean, keyword-rich, hyphenated

### Content Elements
- **Introduction:** Hook + keyword + value proposition (150-200 words)
- **Main Body:**
  - Factual information (dates, venues, history)
  - Ticket purchasing guide
  - Price ranges and categories
  - Seating charts (for venues)
  - Travel tips
  - FAQ section
  - Related matches/venues/teams
- **Conclusion:** Summary + CTA to buy tickets (100-150 words)

### Keyword Strategy
- **Primary Keyword:** In H1, first paragraph, meta description, URL
- **Secondary Keywords:** Variations throughout content (2-3% density)
- **LSI Keywords:** Related terms naturally integrated
- **Internal Links:** 5-10 links to related articles
- **External Links:** 2-3 authoritative sources (FIFA.com, venue sites)

### SEO Best Practices
- **Readability:** Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences), bullet points, subheadings
- **Images:** Alt text with keywords, compressed for speed
- **Schema Markup:** Event, SportsEvent, Place schemas
- **Mobile Optimization:** Responsive content, fast load times
- **E-E-A-T:** Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
