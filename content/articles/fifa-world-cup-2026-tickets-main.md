## H2: Tournament Overview - The First 48-Team World Cup

### H3: Historic Expansion and Format

The FIFA World Cup 2026 marks a revolutionary moment in football history as the tournament expands from 32 to 48 teams. This expansion creates 16 additional spots for nations to qualify, providing unprecedented opportunities for countries from every confederation to compete on football's biggest stage. The new format features 12 groups of four teams each, followed by a knockout stage beginning with the Round of 32.

### H3: Key Tournament Facts

- **Total Teams:** 48 nations from all six continental confederations
- **Total Matches:** 104 games over 39 days (increased from 64 matches)
- **Host Countries:** United States (11 venues), Mexico (3 venues), Canada (2 venues)
- **Host Cities:** 16 cities across three nations
- **Opening Match:** June 11, 2026, at Estadio Azteca, Mexico City
- **Final:** July 19, 2026, at MetLife Stadium, New York/New Jersey
- **Tournament Duration:** 39 days of non-stop football action
- **Expected Attendance:** Over 5 million spectators across all venues
- **Estimated TV Audience:** 5+ billion viewers worldwide

### H3: Group Stage Structure

The 48 teams will be divided into 12 groups (Groups A through L) with four teams in each group. Each team plays three group-stage matches, with the top two teams from each group advancing to the knockout stage, along with the eight best third-placed teams. This format ensures a total of 72 group-stage matches, providing fans with unprecedented access to world-class football.

## H2: Travel Planning for FIFA World Cup 2026

### H3: Visa Requirements

**United States**
- ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization) required for visa waiver countries
- Apply at least 72 hours before travel
- Valid for 2 years, multiple entries
- Cost: $21

**Mexico**
- FMM (Forma Migratoria Múltiple) tourist card required
- Free at border or airport
- Valid for 180 days
- Some nationalities require visa - check before booking

**Canada**
- ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization) required for visa-exempt countries
- Apply online, instant or within 72 hours
- Valid for 5 years
- Cost: CAD $7

### H3: Transportation Between Host Cities

**Air Travel**
- Major airports in all 16 host cities
- Domestic flights most efficient for long distances
- Book early for best prices (3-6 months advance)
- Consider multi-city tickets

**Ground Transportation**
- USA: Amtrak trains, Greyhound buses, rental cars
- Mexico: ADO buses (excellent service), domestic flights
- Canada: VIA Rail, excellent intercity bus service

**Local Transportation**
- Most venues accessible via public transit
- Stadium shuttles available on match days
- Uber/Lyft widely available in North American cities
- Consider staying near venue or public transit

### H3: Accommodation Strategies

**Book Early** (12+ months in advance)
- Hotels near venues fill quickly
- Prices increase dramatically closer to tournament
- Consider staying slightly outside city centers

**Alternative Accommodations**
- Airbnb/VRBO for groups and families
- University dorms (summer vacancy)
- Extended-stay hotels for multi-match visits

**Budget Considerations**
- Expect 2-3x normal room rates during match days
- Bundle packages often include accommodation
- Consider staying in nearby cities with easy transit
